Penne Al Baffo

Description

A swiftly done creamy tomato pasta with ham!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta, preferably penne
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 3 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 8 ounces of deli ham, cubed
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of tomato passata
  • 3 tbsp of parsley, minced
  • 1 cup of reserved pasta water

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until it is al dente
  2. Saut√© the onions for about 3 minutes in butter and olive oil
  3. Add the ham and cook for a few minutes
  4. Add the tomato passata to the pan and bring the sauce to a simmer
  5. After about 5 minutes add the cream, stir to combine and continue to gently simmer
  6. Add the boiled pasta to the pen and mix well to coat
  7. Finish boiling the pasta in the sauce to your liking
  8. Add pasta water as needed
  9. Add salt and pepper as needed
  10. Garnish with parsley and enjoy
