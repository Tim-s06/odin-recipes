Onionpasta

Description

A vegetarian low-effort pasta recipe done in the oven!

Ingredients

  • 5 Small yellow onions, sliced
  • 1 Head of garlic, top sliced off
  • 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 Tbsp of sun dried tomato oil
  • 1 tsp of each salt, pepper, paprika and dried parsley
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  • 3 cups cooked pasta
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 Handful of fresh parsley
  • 1 Handful of fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C)
  2. Add the onions, sundried tomatoes and spices to an ovensafe dish
  3. Toss and place the garlic head in the middle, then drizzle with the oil from the sundried tomatoes
  4. Cover with aluminium foil and place in the oven for 1 hour tossing after 30 minutes
  5. Cook the pasta in salted water around 20 minutes before the onions are done and reserve a bit of pasta water
  6. Remove the garlic head and squeeze out the cloves
  7. Add pasta, coconut milk, juiced lemon, fresh parsley and basil to the dish and toss to combine
  8. Finally add pasta water as needed
  9. Serve hot and enjoy
