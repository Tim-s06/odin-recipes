Chicken-Mozzarella-Pasta

Description

A quick and easy pasta recipe with spicy chicken, veggies and mozzarella!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Chicken breast
  • Tomato paste
  • Spices
  • Cream
  • Spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic, shallot and chicken breast
  2. Cook the pasta in salted water
  3. Saut√© the shallot and garlic in olive oil on medium heat
  4. Add the chicken breast and the spices and cook until the chicken is halfway done
  5. Now add the tomate paste and cook it for 3 minutes
  6. Add the cream and stir to combine
  7. Add the spinach in badges being careful not to overcrowd the pan
  8. Finally add cherry tomatoes, the pasta and some shredded mozzarella
  9. Serve hot and enjoy
